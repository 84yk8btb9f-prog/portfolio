## What it is

Helpmarq is a feedback marketplace. You upload a project — an app, a landing page, a product idea, a pitch deck — and real users give you structured, multi-perspective feedback so you can improve it.

Not generic opinions. Structured feedback across dimensions that actually matter: usability, positioning, first impressions, conversion, copy. The kind of signal you'd normally pay a consultant for, surfaced from real users who actually look at your thing.

## How it works

1. Upload your project with a brief description
2. Define what kind of feedback you're looking for
3. Users with relevant context review and respond using a structured format
4. You get actionable, organized feedback — not noise
