## What it is

Helpmarq is a customer engagement intelligence platform. It ingests support tickets, user sessions, and product events to surface churn risk and expansion signals.

## Current status

- Core data pipeline live
- Engagement scout agent in beta
- Frontend dashboard in progress

## Stack

Next.js frontend, Supabase, Python ML microservices.
