## What it is

A Telegram bot that acts as a task inbox. Send it anything — "remind me to follow up with Alex on Friday", "add buy flight tickets to my errands", "what's on my list for this week" — and it handles the rest. No manual data entry, no app switching.

Supports 5 intents: create task, list tasks, complete task, set reminder, query by tag or date. Claude extracts structure (title, due date, tags, priority) from natural language and writes it to Notion.

## Current status

Deployed and running at `n8n.helpmarq.com`.

## Stack

n8n, Telegram Bot API, OpenRouter (Claude), Notion API.
