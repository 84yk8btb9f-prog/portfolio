## What it is

Creator Roast is an AI tool for creators who are stuck. You paste your profile, it tells you exactly what's broken and what to fix. No sugar-coating.

Whether you're an Instagram creator stuck at 5k, a TikToker whose videos die at 200 views, a LinkedIn professional posting into the void, or an X writer with 800 followers — Creator Roast surfaces the specific problems holding you back.

## What it roasts

- Profile and bio clarity
- Content hooks and opening seconds
- Posting consistency and patterns
- Niche definition and positioning
- Engagement-to-follower ratio analysis
- What successful accounts in your niche do differently

## Why it exists

Most creator advice is either too generic ("post more") or too expensive (hire a coach). Creator Roast gives you specific, honest, actionable feedback at a fraction of the cost.

## Current status

Under active development. Started building April 2026 alongside MarketMyApp. Each roast produces a shareable card with the audit results — designed to spread organically.
