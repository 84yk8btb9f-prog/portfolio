## What it is

A suite of lead scraping tools covering the three main B2B sources: Google Maps (local businesses by category and location), Instagram (profiles matching niche criteria), and X (accounts by keyword, bio, or engagement pattern).

Built as a lower-cost, more controllable alternative to Apify for high-volume lead generation at AutomateSphere.

## Stack

Python, Playwright, Instaloader.
