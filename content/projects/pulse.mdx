## What it is

Pulse is an iOS health and wellness app. It pairs a native SwiftUI client with a Next.js + Supabase relay backend that handles data persistence, sync, and any server-side logic.

Bundle ID: `app.pulse.ios`

## Current status

Active development.

## Stack

SwiftUI (iOS), Next.js, Supabase.
