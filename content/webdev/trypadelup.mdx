## Overview

TryPadelUp is the public-facing site and court booking platform for padel venues in Greece. Fast, mobile-first, and built to convert.

## What I built

- Marketing landing page with venue showcase
- Court availability and booking flow
- Admin dashboard for venue operators

## Stack

Next.js 15, Tailwind CSS, Supabase (auth + bookings).
